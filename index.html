About the Instructor
Professor Chris Rosa
Adjunct Professor, Computer Science
Sacred Heart University

"While shipping a product that makes a difference is immensely satisfying, watching a student connect to the material for the first time is something else entirely. I’ve been fortunate to experience both."

Drawing from a career as a founding CTO and software executive spanning hands-on engineering, team building, and organizational leadership, Chris has found his most purposeful work in the university classroom. As an adjunct professor of Computer Science, he brings real-world perspective to the next generation of technologists — making complex concepts tangible and connecting theory to the realities of building real-life solutions.
